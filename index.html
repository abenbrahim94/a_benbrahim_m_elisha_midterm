Why you should not have a dog.

Adopting a dog seems like a very sweet thing to do, but in reality your life is about to change for years to come. Sure a puppy is adorable and they are great to snuggle with, but there are numerous downfalls that no one wants to talk about. Here are some of the worst things no one tells you about having a dog.

1. Your schedule now revolves around an animal
2. They are way more expensive than you expected
3. Cutting their nails is the most terrifying experience in life
4. They are going to wreck your furniture
5. Every window in your home will be smudged
6. If your dog gets fleas, you might as well burn your house down
7. He is going to die soon

Why have a dog.

Dogs are worth having because they can support a person in so many ways.A dog can change a lazy person to be more active and adventurous. For people who have trouble showing their emotions or having trouble interacting with other people, having a dog that is going to be loyal to you and always be with you is a great thing. Some people struggle to have better security around their property, a dog can also be helpful in those cases. If you want to raise a confident child, give them a dog. It has been shown that dog owners who have an attachment to their pet have a stronger attachment to their environment and community, making them more comfortable with where they are and eventually who they are. These are the few reasons why having a dog is important and a must have.
