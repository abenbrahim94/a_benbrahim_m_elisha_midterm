Why you should not have a dog.

Adopting a dog seems like a very sweet thing to do, but in reality your life is about to change for years to come. Sure a puppy is adorable and they are great to snuggle with, but there are numerous downfalls that no one wants to talk about. Here are some of the worst things no one tells you about having a dog.

Your schedule now revolves around an animal

They are way more expensive than you expected

Cutting their nails is the most terrifying experience in life

They are going to wreck your furniture

Every window in your home will be smudged

If your dog gets fleas, you might as well burn your house down

He is going to die soon
